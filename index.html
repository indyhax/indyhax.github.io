About IndyHAX

Welcome to IndyHAX, an innovative hackathon hosted at Independence High School! This event brings together creative minds to build cutting-edge solutions that leverage the power of Artificial Intelligence to improve student life.

Event Details

Location: Independence High School

Date: To Be Determined (TBD)

Focus: AI-Powered Student Tools

The Challenge

Build an AI-Powered Student Assignment & To-Do Tracker

Students are challenged to create a web-based platform that helps their peers manage their academic life more effectively.

Platform Requirements

  • To-Do List Management: An intuitive interface for students to track their tasks
  • Assignment Tracking: Easy-to-use tools for monitoring homework, projects, and deadlines
  • AI Integration: The core feature - an AI Agent that enhances the user experience

AI Agent Requirements

Participants must build an AI Agent using resources from industry-leading providers:

  • Google AI Resources (such as Gemini API, Google Cloud AI)
  • Microsoft AI Resources (such as Azure AI, Copilot API)

AI Features to Consider

  • Smart task prioritization
  • Deadline reminders and intelligent scheduling
  • Assignment difficulty assessment
  • Study time recommendations
  • Natural language interaction for adding tasks
  • Personalized productivity insights

Presentation & Judging

Teams will present their projects to a panel of judges, demonstrating:

  • Functionality: How the AI-powered platform works
  • Innovation: Creative use of AI technology
  • Impact: How this solution would change student life
  • Technical Excellence: Quality of implementation and AI integration
  • User Experience: Ease of use and design

Our Vision

We envision a future where AI assists students in managing their academic responsibilities more effectively, allowing them to:

  • Spend less time worrying about deadlines and more time learning
  • Receive personalized support that adapts to their unique needs
  • Develop better organizational habits through intelligent assistance
  • Reduce academic stress through proactive planning

IndyHAX is more than a competition - it's an opportunity to be at the forefront of AI innovation in education, to solve real problems that you and your peers face every day, and to demonstrate how technology can empower students to succeed.
