Frequently Asked Questions

► What is a hackathon?
A hackathon is an event where people collaborate intensively on software projects over a short period of time. It's a mix of "hack" (meaning creative problem-solving, not breaking into systems) and "marathon." Participants form teams, brainstorm ideas, code solutions, and present their projects - all in a fun, fast-paced environment!
► Is there a cost to participate?
No! IndyHAX is completely free to attend. There are no registration fees, admission costs, or hidden charges. Just bring yourself, your laptop, and your enthusiasm!
► Who can participate?
IndyHAX is open to all ages! Whether you're in middle school, high school, or beyond, everyone is welcome to participate. Students, hobbyists, and anyone interested in learning and building are encouraged to join!
► Do I need to be an expert programmer?
Not at all! IndyHAX welcomes participants of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner just learning to code or have some experience, you'll find ways to contribute. You can work on design, project ideas, presentations, or learn from your teammates. The goal is to learn and build something together!
► How do teams work at IndyHAX?
Teams typically have 2-5 members with diverse skills. You can form teams before the event or find teammates on the day of. Good teams include a mix of roles: coders, designers, idea generators, and presenters. Collaboration and communication are just as important as technical skills!
► What should I bring to IndyHAX?
Bring your laptop (fully charged!), chargers, any necessary cables, and a positive attitude. Most importantly, bring your creativity and willingness to learn. Don't worry about having everything figured out in advance!
► What if I've never been to a hackathon before?
First-timers are always welcome! IndyHAX is designed to be a beginner-friendly learning experience. Mentors and organizers will be there to help you. You'll meet other students, learn new technologies, and gain experience working on real projects. Everyone starts somewhere, and IndyHAX is perfect for beginners!
► How long does IndyHAX last?
The event duration will be announced closer to the date. The timeline will typically include an opening ceremony where the challenge is revealed, hacking time to build your project, and then presentations and judging at the end. You'll have enough time to build something meaningful!
► Can I use AI in my project?
Absolutely! AI is not only allowed but encouraged at IndyHAX. You can use AI tools, APIs, and technologies like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Microsoft Azure AI, or any other AI resources to enhance your project. However, AI is completely optional - you can build amazing projects without it too!
► What can I learn from participating?
IndyHAX will teach you practical skills like coding, using APIs, teamwork, time management, and presenting ideas. You'll also gain exposure to new technologies, meet fellow students interested in tech, build your portfolio, and discover what you're capable of creating in a short time. Plus, it's fun!
► Are there prizes?
Yes! IndyHAX will offer prizes for winning teams. Beyond the prizes though, the real rewards are the experience, skills, and connections you'll gain from participating. Everyone who joins and learns something new is a winner!
